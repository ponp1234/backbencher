🌍 Diversity in Nature

Discover the amazing variety of life around us!

What is Diversity? 🌈

Diversity means having many different types of things. In nature, we see diversity everywhere!

Just like how people look different and have different skills, animals and plants are also very different from each other.

🤔 Did You Know?
There are over 8.7 million different species of animals and plants on Earth! That's a lot of diversity!

Diverse Habitats 🏠

Different animals and plants live in different places called habitats:

🌊

🌲
Forest
Bears, deer, and tall trees make their home here
🏜️
Desert
Camels, lizards, and cacti survive in hot, dry places

Why is Diversity Important? 🤝

Diversity in nature is super important because:

🔄 Animals and plants help each other survive

🌍 Different species keep our planet healthy and balanced

🍯 Bees help flowers make seeds, flowers give bees nectar

🌱 Animals spread plant seeds to new places

🌟 Remember!
Every living thing, no matter how small, plays an important role in nature!
