Help & Support

Find answers to common questions and learn how to navigate the Backbench platform.

Getting Started

Here a quick guide on setting up your account and accessing various features:

  • Registration: Visit the registration page and fill in the required information. (Join the Adventure! link in login page)
  • Login: Use your username and password to access your account.

Taking Exams

Follow these steps to start and complete exams:

  • Select Exam: Choose an exam from the catalog on the homepage.
  • Answer Questions: Complete each question, selecting or entering your answer.
  • Submit Exam: Once youve finished all questions, submit the exam to see your score.

Viewing Past Attempts

You can view all your past exam attempts and scores by visiting the "Past Attempts" page.

Profile & Settings

Manage your account and preferences in the profile and settings sections:

  • Update Profile: Edit your username or other personal details.
